Mission

While all members of Congress possess the same amount of power, some simply exercise that power less than others. In order to mitigate political inactivity, Congress Activity monitors the voting behavior of every member of the United States Congress. A high absentee rate points to a congress member's lack of political willâ€” missed votes are wasted chances to push for what their agendas promised when they were voted into office.

API

Congress Activity uses data from the ProPublica Congress API. Propublica posseses voting data from 1991 for the House of Representatives and from 1989 for the Senate.
